Chabot's CHC

Chabot's Computer Hardware Club

We make things practical!

Our club's primary objective is to help each other learn and discuss about computer hardware and technology-related topics.



Some of the stuff that we do are:

  • Building PCs
  • Computer Safety
  • Software Installation
  • Talking about Hardware-related Topics
  • and more!


  • We meet every other Tuesday @ the Chabot College Library (Building 100, 2nd Floor) from 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM

    • Due to the COVID-19 Pandemic, we will be meeting on Discord from now on.



    Requirements:

  • MUST be enrolled in at least one unit @ Chabot College
